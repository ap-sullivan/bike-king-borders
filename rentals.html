EXCEPTIONAL VALUE BIKE RENTALS

We offer a wide range of mountain and road bike rentals for periods of half day to two weeks. We also offer electric bike rental for a maximum of two days. All our rental bikes are less than a year old and are kept in tip-top condition by our expert in house servicing team. Please note that due to health and safety we can’t offer electric bikes to anyone under the age of 16.
